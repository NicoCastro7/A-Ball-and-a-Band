About Me

I'm Nicolás Castro, a 17-year-old Colombian living in Argentina. Music is my daily companion and a powerful form of creative expression. Soccer, deeply rooted in my family, became even more meaningful when I saw how passionately Argentinians live the game. This site is my way of showing what moves me.

Soccer

I support Independiente Santa Fe, a Colombian team full of history and heart. Being away from my country made me feel soccer in a new way — it became a bridge back home, a fire that never goes out.

Music

From the poetic soul of Spinetta to the genius of Cerati, the raw honesty of Charly García and the power of Fito Páez — Argentinian rock shaped how I feel, think, and create. These artists aren’t just music, they’re memory.
